- **Pedagogical Content Knowledge (PCK)** means knowing how to teach subject content. - a blend of pedagogy and subject knowledge.
	- Understanding where misunderstanding certain topics come from - knowing why they're easy/hard.
	- Knowledge of the relationship between content and the children - common conceptions, misconceptions, and difficulties.
	- Knowledge of the relationship between content and the curriculum - sequence and structure of content.
	- Knowledge of the relationship between content and teaching - strategies, pedagogical techniques
1. Knowledge of the relationship between content and teaching – the specific teaching strategies, pedagogical techniques and ways of representing, modelling, and explaining knowledge that can be used to address children’s learning needs in particular classroom circumstances with specific content.
