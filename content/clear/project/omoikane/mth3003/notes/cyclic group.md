# Cyclic Group

A **cyclic group** is defined by a group $G$ with operation $*$. We say that $G$ is a cyclic group if it can be generated by a generator element $g\in G:g^n=\underbrace{g*g*\dots*g}_{n\text{ times}}$, where $g^0=e_{G}$ and $g^{-n}=(g^n)^{-1}=(g^{-1})^n$. We write this as $\langle g \rangle\leq G$, such that $|\langle g\rangle|=o(g)$.
