# Relative Error

**Relative error** is the absolute error divided by the magnitude of the exact value.

$$
E_{\text{rel}} = \frac{|y_{\text{approx}} - y_{\text{exact}}|}{|y_{\text{exact}}|}
$$

Relative error is dimensionless and scale-independent, making it more meaningful than [[Absolute error]] when comparing errors across quantities of very different sizes. It expresses the error as a fraction of the true value.
